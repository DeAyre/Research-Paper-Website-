Current Use & Benefits

Currently Self-driving cars or AVs (Autonomous Vehicle) are being used as robot taxis and features in tesla models. There are companies such as Waymo (Google sister company), (Tucker, 2021) and Motional (Hyundai sister company) who are creating a fleet of robot taxis for ride share apps in major cities (Avery, 2020).



Companies saw the need for more autonomous vehicles due to the pandemic that has struck the world. This gives the ability for there to be less human contact while ridesharing and allows for more efficient/ reliable transportation while also making the rider feel safer with them being the car alone rather than riding in a car with a stranger.



There also are more commercial options for AV such as Tesla's who combine both environmentally friendly electric vehicles with autonomous vehicles all at an affordable price (well semi-affordable price). Tesla’s Self-driving feature was built with reducing the amount of human error in mind (Tesla). The number of car crashes that happen due to DUI’s and people not paying attention (aka texting while driving) could lower if more AVs were on the road, making driving less risky for human lives (Segarra, 2021).
