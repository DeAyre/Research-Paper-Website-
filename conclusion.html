Conclusion

Self-driving cars are Autonomous Vehicles (aka robotic cars) that can drive by themselves with little to no human interaction. Even though they sound like promising innovative technology there are still many issues that effects the use of these cars such as security aspects like technical issues and crashes reported on the news, ethical aspects of selfdriving cars being safe and affordable also social aspects of people wanting to trust their lives in the hands of software.

If the next 10 years play out in favor of AVs more improvements will create a vehicle that drives safer than a human driver. AVs will soon become the mainstream vehicle to take the roads in either the form of robot-taxis or commercial vehicles that will be the future of transportation. The journey to create a sustainable, reliable, and safe vehicle is only a short drive away.
