Social Aspects

Would you trust your lives in the hands of software? Do you think that AVs are safer than human driven cars?



Would you ride an AV to your destination? Some may say “NO” others may choose a “Yes.” A reported poll taken by Morning Consult, (Teale, 2022) back in 2021 shows that only 17% of U.S adults believe that AVs are safer than human driving vehicles. While an additional 37% of U.S adults said would ride in an AV (Teale, 2022). Growing trust in these vehicles tends to dwindle whenever there is a reported crash of an AV even if it is a human error accident.

Public perception over time will be a slow progress as AV’s are constantly being improved from the data collect and simulations being ran on them (Segarra, 2021). Public perception may grow to trust these vehicles in the future, or it may not only time and technology will tell.
