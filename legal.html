Legal Implications

One question many are asking is the implication of an AV crashing and what it could mean. Such as who is liable for the damage cost? The company, the person, the car itself? How does insurance charge you for the damages if there are some?



Even though the whole point of AV’s were intended for safety, but accidents still happen and can happen at any time. These are the issues surrounding AVs to this day and still is a constant nuisance for lawmakers around the world trying to produce rules for these new types of vehicles to ensure everyone’s safety. With so many companies promising to unveiled new AV’s it a scramble for lawmakers to keep up with current technology and its legal limits. With safety in mind car companies test out these self-driving features in computer simulated environments or on the public roads (Segarra, 2021).



Any data collected from these tests are sent to government and local authorities to better collect data and form better ways of creating rules for these vehicles and gain a better understanding of how these vehicles interact with the real world (Segarra, 2021). Even with the data collected lawmakers will still have to keep in mind the risk associated with AV’s and the amount of people willing to trust their lives in the hand of a machine who cannot think ethically like a man
