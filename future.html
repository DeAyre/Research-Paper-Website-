Future Aspects

The possibilities of this technology if perfected could be endless, AVs has the potential of lowering the amount of car accidents people may die from, change the reliability of transportation, and create a more sustainable way of living by only running on electricity rather than gasoline (Cusack, 2021).



In order for AVs to become more mainstream more work needs to be done, specifically on maneuvering around an unpredictable environment, getting passengers calmly to point A and point B, and gain the public’s trust into being safer than human drivers (having very little accidents) (Segarra, 2021). Regulation from both governments and Insurance agencies will both need to chance as well as cities and towns infrastructure, highways and roads will have to undergo some changes to ensure a smooth transition to AV’s. With these challenges ahead more companies are more determined to push out these AVs within the next ten years to become a mainstream source of transportation (Cusack, 2021).
